Favourite Games

The Witcher 3

The Witcher 3 is one of my favourite games of all time. It has a really good and long story, amazing graphics and I simply love its atmosphere. This game taught me how different decisions in our lives can bring sometimes unexpected consequences.

Life is Strange

This game is my favourite alongside The Witcher 3, it's really hard for me to say which one is better. Life is Strange taught me about choices and consequences in a really emotional way. The characters are very ordinary and imperfect people that I got very connected with and even related to. It showed me how important it is to enjoy your life as much as you can with the people you love.

NieR: Automata

I really love this game. Its story is beautiful for bringing up an existential philosophy about androids which only reason to "live" is to fight for humanity.
